Usage

  • Use the assets as-is: don't recolor, distort, rotate, or add effects.
  • Prefer SVG wherever possible; PNGs are provided for contexts that need raster.
  • Use the circle variants on busy backgrounds, transparent variants on flat ones.
  • Don't use the Tunecamp name or logo to imply endorsement of your product or instance.
